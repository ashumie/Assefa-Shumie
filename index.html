CODE OF CONDUCT

This code of conduct reflects my work ethical relationship with clients, coworkers, vendors, business partners and society.

  • I shall keep all information confidential. However, such information may be disclosed to a third party only up on request. Under such circumstance, the client will be required to sign a Release of Information form.
  • I will respect other professional colleagues' work and I will not replicate their work.
  • I will be transparent and honest with clients, coworkers, and business partners.
  • I will use the best and most up-to-date standards available on web design technology.
  • I will always act in the best interest of my clients.
  • I will meet my contractual agreement with my clients within the agreed timeframe.
  • I will be transparent with my clients when signing the contractual agreement that stipulate fees, the nature of project, and timeframe in which the project is to be completed.
  • I am committed to completing projects on time as stipulated on the contractual agreement.
  • My clients can withdraw their service requests at anytime. In this instance, the client will be asked to sign a withdrawal of service form.
  • I will promote inclusiveness, diversity, fairness, and impartiality.
  • I will examine the content of my work to ensure it causes no harm to users and society as a whole.
  • I will receive fair compensation for my work as per contractual agreement.
  • My compensation will be paid fully when my work is complete to the satisfaction of my client.
  • I will respect the intellectual property rights of other professionals and collaborators.
  • I will not use other designers' work without their consent and permission.
  • I will educate my clients about intellectual property rights.
